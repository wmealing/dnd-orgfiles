*** Jump
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: jump
:END:
/1st-level transmutation/

*Casting Time:* 1 action

*Range:* Touch

*Components:* V, S, M (a grasshopper's hind leg)

*Duration:* 1 minute

You touch a creature. The creature's jump distance is tripled until the
spell ends.
